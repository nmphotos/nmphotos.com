Wedding photography is an art. Its about capturing emotions. Its about seeing the little things. Its candid and its posed. And its gotta look fantastic!

My approach to wedding photography is to take plenty of candid shots. I take posed shots too, but its often the photo where the bride's turning away and laughing that really captures the moment. I shoot colour and black and white film, and I can also do digital. I always provide the negatives to the couple, so there’s no extra costs after the wedding. I also provide each couple with a digital montage print combining photos from the day.
